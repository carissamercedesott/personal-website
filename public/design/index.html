Design system

Every page on this site renders from these exact tokens! View the source.

Concept

Warm pastels, crisp surfaces, soft motion.

Two token layers: named color sets (primitives) that never change, and semantic tokens that remap between light and dark.

Color palette

Seven families, one job each — three core families carry the site, four support families exist for tags and status. Expand a family for its full ramp: every shade is there for a reason, and the reason is usually contrast. Click any shade to copy its hex.

Components never use these values directly — they go through the semantic tokens below. Figma naming: Blush/500, Oat/100, …

Semantic tokens

Components only use these — never raw palette values. Toggle the theme above and watch them remap.

Typography

IBM Plex Sans across display, headings, and body (700 for display/bold, 600 for headings), with IBM Plex Mono for accents. Body line-height 1.6; headings 1.15 with −2% tracking.

Motion

Four durations, three easings, three named moves — rise for entrances, settle for hovers (fast in, soft out), pop for dialogs. Springs ride a linear() curve, transform-only. With prefers-reduced-motion set, everything on this site renders in place — including these demos.
