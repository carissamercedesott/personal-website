Waddl

A cozy desktop companion for Claude Code — a mini Electron UI that lets you watch an AI agent waddle through your code.

AI Agents Electron TypeScript

Waddl is a pocket-sized desktop companion for Claude Code. Instead of a full terminal, you get a cozy little window where an AI agent waddles through your codebase — reading files, making edits, and narrating what it's up to as it goes.

Built with Electron and TypeScript as a playground for exploring what a friendlier, more approachable agent UI could feel like.

🛠️ Personal project — in progress

Cross Math

A math puzzle game live in the Duolingo iOS app — constraint-programming puzzle generation, shipped to 100M+ users.

Swift Algorithms Product

A math puzzle game shipped inside the Duolingo iOS app. I built the constraint-programming algorithm that generates accurate puzzle numbers, the backend algorithm that produces random puzzle shapes in real time, and responsive Swift UI that scales across all iOS devices.

I presented the business case to senior leadership, gained CEO approval, and launched it to Duolingo's full audience.

📱 Live in Duolingo on iOS — 100M+ users
